Pesto pasta salad

Description

Pesto pasta salad is a vibrant and flavorful dish that's perfect for any occasion. It starts with tender, al dente pasta—often fusilli, rotini, or penne—that’s tossed with a zesty, aromatic pesto sauce made from fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. The result is a creamy, herbaceous coating that clings to each pasta shape. To enhance the dish, you can add a variety of ingredients such as sun-dried tomatoes, cherry tomatoes, black olives, or diced bell peppers for extra color and flavor. A sprinkle of toasted pine nuts or additional Parmesan cheese on top adds a delightful crunch and richness. For a touch of freshness, a handful of baby spinach or arugula can be folded in just before serving. This pasta salad is a perfect blend of savory, tangy, and fresh flavors, making it a great side dish or a light main course. It’s also versatile and can be served chilled or at room temperature, making it an ideal choice for picnics, potlucks, or casual gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Pasta: 12 ounces fusilli or other spiral pasta
  • Tomatoes: 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved.
  • Arugula: 2 cups packed arugula.
  • Pesto: 1 cup homemade pesto or 1 (6.5-ounce) jar purchased basil pesto
  • Cheese:
    • 1/2 cup fresh pearl mozzarella balls or cubed feta cheese
    • 1/4 cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese
  • Mayonnaise: 2 tablespoons mayonnaise.
  • Lemon juice: 1 to 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Seasonings:
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt.
    • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper.

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook fusilli in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  3. Rinse pasta under cold water to cool.
  4. Combine cooked pasta, tomatoes, arugula, pesto, mozzarella, Parmesan, mayonnaise, and lemon juice in a large mixing bowl. Stir to combine.
  5. Add reserved pasta water as needed to thin out the pesto. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve immediately, or cover and store in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
